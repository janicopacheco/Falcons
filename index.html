Freight forwarding

is what we do

Sharing logistics expertise



Established in 2022, Falcons Freight Forwarding Services positions itself to be a
world-class provider of logistics services and solutions. We are a full-service
commercial international freight forwarding firm based in Manila, Philippines that
focuses on servicing customers with global sourcing and logistics need.
With our dedicated professionals, our resolute is to serve and cater our existing and future
clients the best way forward.



Our Mission and Vision

To become the leader of providing logistics services worldwide - adhering to
constant innovative solutions, global quality standards and logistics best practices.

To provide the most trusted, expert and reliable international freight
forwarding services in the world.

Falcons aims to provide our customers with the best service across all their needs.
We build legacy towards our goal of delivering out logistics expertise and excellent management.
